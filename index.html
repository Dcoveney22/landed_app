How Landed Works

Landed takes your product range and runs each SKU through a full UK market value chain, assessing whether your pricing is retail-ready and flagging anything that needs attention before you go to market.

The data you have provided to your account manager is used to build your product profile - including brand, category, ABV, size and ex-works price. From there, Landed does the rest.

How it works

  • Your ex-works price is assessed against the current HMRC duty rate for your product's ABV bracket
  • Logistics costs are factored in based on your product's country of origin
  • A retail margin is applied to model a UK retail price for each SKU
  • Any product whose modelled price falls outside advised category thresholds is flagged for review

Price Check

Once the pipeline has run, click Price Check to see a full list of any SKUs that Landed recommends reviewing before release. This gives you the opportunity to assess your pricing strategy before your products reach the market.

To update your product data or add new SKUs to your range, speak to your account manager.
